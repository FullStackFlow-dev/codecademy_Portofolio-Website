Who am I

From vampire to software engineer, what an incredible journey it has been so far!

Yes, you read that correctly. I was born and raised in the wild, mysterious mountains of Transylvania. And no, no need to clutch your garlic, I am not a vampire. Quite the opposite, in fact. With a professional background in Health and Social Care, I was often the one left feeling completely drained (no fangs involved - just long hours, big hearts, and plenty of tea).

That experience shaped me into someone who's resilient, deeply empathetic, and a firm believer in the power of human connection (and a good laugh). Whether I'm supporting others, solving problems, or navigating a challenge, I bring warmth, wit, and a strong work ethic along with a bit of Transylvanian charm.
Passionate about building the future, line by line. This isn't just about landing a job, it's about building a new identity. I'm not just learning to code, I'm preparing to shift careers, mindsets, and life paths. I'm fuelled by curiosity, discipline, and a stubborn belief that it's never too late to reinvent yourself.

Beyond technology, my favourite code to crack is life itself. You will often find me casually debugging it with a cup of tea and Netflix, finding peace in nature, or making memories with friends and family.
